Jasmine flowers are known for their delicate appearance and sweet fragrance. These blooms can be found in various colors, including white, yellow, and pink, and they often symbolize purity, love, and beauty. Jasmine plants are typically vining or shrubby and thrive in warm climates, making them popular choices for gardens and landscaping. They are widely used in perfumes, teas, and traditional medicine due to their aromatic qualities and potential health benefits.
