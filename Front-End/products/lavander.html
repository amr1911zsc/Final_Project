lavander flowers

Lavender (Lavandula) is a highly aromatic and versatile flowering plant known for its vibrant purple flowers and calming fragrance. It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is native to the Mediterranean region. Lavender has been cultivated for centuries for its beauty, fragrance, and numerous practical uses.

Lavandin

A hybrid of English and Portuguese lavender, known for its larger size and strong scent. Varieties: "Grosso," "Provence," "Phenomenal.".
